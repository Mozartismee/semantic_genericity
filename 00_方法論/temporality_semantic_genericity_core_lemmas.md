## 1. Family T — Temporality Generates Universality

### Lemma T1（將來性生成普遍性 · Futurity generates universality）

**中文敘述**

在時間性現象學的框架下，一個結構在某一範圍內被視為具備「existence/uniqueness 型的普遍性」，並非因為它在概念上帶有天賦本質，而是因為主體以其將來性反覆承諾：凡被視為屬於某一類的後續經驗，皆須經由該結構作為中介來被理解與實作，並在實務上排除競爭性的中介路徑。

**English formulation**

Given a temporally extended subject, a structure $u$ is said to have an *existence-uniqueness type universality* within a domain $D$ not in virtue of any intrinsic conceptual essence, but in virtue of the subject's futural commitments: for every future instance in $D$ that is taken up as "one of these cases", the uptake is systematically factored through $u$, while competing mediations are practically excluded.

**Remark**

層級：時間構形層（主體時間結構）。  
用途：區分「被時間生成的普遍性」與「被預設為概念本質的普遍性」，後續 T2, S1–S3 皆依賴此 lemma。

---

### Lemma T2（過去節點作為通往自我敘事的 universal object）

**中文敘述**

在自我敘事視為一個範疇的模型中，給定一個過去節點 $t_0$（例如創傷事件、強烈羞恥、某次關鍵評價），若主體在其後的時間裡，將所有與某一主題相關、並且「被算作關於我」的經驗，皆解讀為先經由 $t_0$ 再進入自我敘事，則 $t_0$ 在該主題所生成的子範疇中扮演類 universal object 的角色。

**English formulation**

Let self-narrative be modeled as a category $\mathcal{C}$ of configurations "of myself". Let $t_0$ be a past configuration (for example a traumatic episode or a decisive humiliation). If, in the subsequent temporal unfolding, every experience that is both thematically related and taken up as "about me in this respect" is interpreted via a factorization through $t_0$ before entering $\mathcal{C}$, then $t_0$ functions as a universal-like object in the generated subcategory for that theme.

**Remark**

層級：時間構形層，附帶敘事結構。  
用途：提供「創傷節點」「關鍵羞恥事件」如何在技術上扮演 universal-like 對象的骨架，供妄念、創傷案例引用。

---

### Lemma T3（妄念與重播：時間 factorization 的自動啟動）

**中文敘述**

若某一過去節點 $t_0$ 已在特定主題上取得 T2 所述的 universal-like 角色，則所謂「妄念」可以被刻畫為：在面對當下刺激時，與該主題相關的時間 factorization 結構自動啟動，使得尚未被充分覺察的當下經驗，優先被拉入「經由 $t_0$」的路徑中，而非允許新的中介節點出場。妄念的頻繁出現，對應於此一 factorization 結構在時間中的高度穩定與過度靈敏。

**English formulation**

If a past node $t_0$ has acquired the universal-like role described in Lemma T2 for a given theme, then what is called "deluded thought" (vexed proliferation) can be characterized as the automatic activation of the corresponding temporal factorization: when confronted with present stimuli, thematically related experiences are preferentially routed through $t_0$ before any alternative mediation can be formed. High frequency of such deluded thoughts corresponds to a highly stabilized and over-sensitive factorization structure in time.

**Remark**

層級：時間構形層與心念層交界。  
用途：連接現象學上「妄念的反覆湧現」與範疇語言中的 factorization 穩定性，為後續邪見與我執的刻畫提供時間面向。

## 2. Family S — Meaningful Thought Implies Semantic Genericity

### Lemma S1（念頭一旦「有意義」，即已被放入一個帶泛性的場域）

**中文敘述**

在語義泛性理論的框架下，一個念頭僅以「雜訊」形式一閃而過時，尚不構成語義對象。一旦主體在某一具體實踐與目標之下承認這個念頭「有意義」、足以被當作理由或判準，則等價於：為它選定了一個限制範疇（對應於該實踐中的 narrative/context/field），並在其中賦予它至少一種語義泛性，即一種在該範圍內可重複被援引的普遍適用角色。

**English formulation**

Within the framework of semantic genericity, a fleeting mental event that remains mere "noise" does not yet count as a semantic object. Once, however, a subject acknowledges a thought as "meaningful" under a concrete practice and aim, in the sense that it may serve as a reason or criterion, this is equivalent to assigning it to a restricted category (the relevant narrative/context/field for that practice) and granting it at least one form of semantic genericity there, that is, a role that can be repeatedly invoked as "applicable across cases" within that domain.

**Remark**

層級：心念層與語義層。  
用途：將「有意義」直接等價成「在某限制範疇中承擔 generic 角色」，是時間性導入語義泛性理論的入口。

---

### Lemma S2（「心有所住」作為多場域多重泛性的標記）

**中文敘述**

若一個念頭不僅在單一實踐中被視為有意義，且主體在多個 narrative/context/field 中反覆默認其為合理的出發點或約束條件，則此狀態可稱為「心有所住」。在語義泛性的語言中，這等價於：同一語句或構形在多個場域中同時取得語義泛性資格，並開始在不同實踐裡扮演收束未來可能性的共用骨架。

**English formulation**

If a thought is not only meaningful within a single practice, but is repeatedly treated by the subject as a legitimate starting point or constraint across several narratives/contexts/fields, the state may be called "clinging" or "dwelling" of mind. In the language of semantic genericity, this is equivalent to the same sentence or configuration simultaneously acquiring generic status in multiple fields, and thereby serving as a shared skeleton that restricts future possibilities across distinct practices.

**Remark**

層級：心念層、語義層、實踐層的接口。  
用途：把傳統「有所住」翻寫成「多場域泛性過度授權」，為邪見與我執的語義刻畫提供基底。

---

### Lemma S3（語義泛性與時間 factorization 的耦合）

**中文敘述**

當一個念頭在某主題上獲得 T2 所述的時間性 universal-like 節點，並同時在該主題相關的多個實踐場域中取得 S2 所述的多重語義泛性時，便形成一種強耦合結構：同一節點一方面決定未來經驗在時間上的 factorization，另一方面作為多場域中「何者算作合理理由」的共同骨架。此時，解除其影響不再只是修正單一信念，而是需要同時鬆動時間結構與語義泛性授權。

**English formulation**

When a single thought or configuration both acquires a temporal universal-like role as in Lemma T2 for a given theme, and gains multi-field semantic genericity as in Lemma S2 across practices related to that theme, a tightly coupled structure arises: the same node governs the factorization of future experiences in time, while also serving as the shared skeleton of "what counts as a legitimate reason" across several fields. Undoing its influence then requires not merely revising an isolated belief, but concurrently loosening both the temporal structure and the granted semantic genericity.

**Remark**

層級：時間構形層與語義層的交疊。  
用途：定義「高能邪見」與「深層創傷信念」的結構條件，為後續治療、修行與語用分析搭建共用語言。

## 3. Family E — Evil View & Self-Clinging as Wrong Genericity

### Lemma E1（邪見作為錯位 universal object 與錯誤泛性）

**中文敘述**

在時間與語義結合的框架下，可將「邪見」工作性地定義為：在不適當的場域與主題上，將某一節點（事件、句子、評價、自我形象）誤封為 universal-like 對象，並賦予它未經審計或過強的語義泛性，使得大量後續經驗被迫經由此節點 factor through，並在多個實踐中以它為收束未來可能性的骨架，而無視其有限的適用域與生成條件。

**English formulation**

Within the combined temporal and semantic framework, "evil view" can be operationally defined as follows: in an inappropriate field and theme, a node (event, sentence, judgment, self-image) is mistakenly sealed as a universal-like object and granted unchecked or excessive semantic genericity, so that a wide range of future experiences are forced to factor through it, and multiple practices rely on it as a skeleton for restricting possibilities, while its limited domain of validity and conditions of emergence are ignored.

**Remark**

層級：時間層、語義層與倫理層交界。  
用途：提供一個非形上學、可審計的邪見定義，使佛學用語與語義泛性理論接軌。

---

### Lemma E2（我執作為「過度泛性化的我」）

**中文敘述**

「我執」可被刻畫為：主體在多個場域與主題中，系統性地將「我」這一標記賦予過度的語義泛性，要求任何被視為關鍵的經驗與判斷，皆必須以「關於我」的方式被記錄與解釋，並允許「我」在時間上佔據大量 universal-like 節點的位置。結果是，「我」不再只是眾多語義角色之一，而是成為跨場域的預設中介。

**English formulation**

Self-clinging can be characterized as a systematic over-extension of the semantic genericity of the marker "I": across many fields and themes, the subject demands that any experience or judgment taken as significant be registered and interpreted "as about me", and allows "I" to occupy many universal-like nodes in time. As a result, "I" ceases to be just one semantic role among others and becomes a default mediator across fields.

**Remark**

層級：心念層與語義層。  
用途：將我執描述為一種泛性錯置，而非僅僅心理傾向，便於與去中心化或無我實踐對話。

---

### Lemma E3（邪禪式正念：壓抑妄念而升級錯誤 universal object）

**中文敘述**

若一種「正念」或修行實踐主要操作為：在不觸及 T2–T3 所述的時間 factorization 結構與 E1–E2 所述的錯位 universal-like 節點的前提下，透過技術手段減少表層妄念與情緒波動，並同時在語義上強化一個新的自我形象（例如「正在覺察的我」「修行得宜的我」），使其在多個場域中取得泛性，則此實踐可被視為「邪禪式正念」：它壓抑表面症狀，同時升級錯誤 universal object 的權限。

**English formulation**

If a form of "mindfulness" or practice operates mainly by reducing surface-level deluded thoughts and emotional fluctuations through techniques, while leaving untouched the temporal factorization structures of Lemmas T2–T3 and the mislocated universal-like nodes of Lemmas E1–E2, and at the same time semantically reinforcing a new self-image (for example "the one who is aware" or "the properly practicing self") so that it acquires generic status across fields, then this practice can be regarded as a "perverted mindfulness": it suppresses symptoms while upgrading the permissions of a wrong universal object.

**Remark**

層級：實踐層與語義層。  
用途：作為診斷標準，用以區分真正鬆動我執與邪見的實踐，與僅僅美化既有錯置結構的實踐。

## 4. Family P — Public Universality Principle

### Lemma P1（公共普遍性原則）

**中文敘述**

凡在公共場域中宣稱某一命題或結構具有「普遍性」，並企圖據此影響決策、規範他人或界定「合理／正常」者，必須說明：該普遍性所依賴的 future-structure 如何從個體主體的時間結構，延拓為場域內多主體可共享的時間結構；若無法提供此種延拓的可審計敘述，則該普遍性至多具有「個人未來性偏好」的地位，不構成公共語義必然性。

**English formulation**

Whenever, in a public setting, a statement or structure is claimed to possess "universality" and is used to influence decisions, regulate others, or define what counts as "reasonable" or "normal", the claimant must explain how the underlying future-structure is extended from an individual temporal configuration to a shared temporal configuration across subjects within the field. If no auditable account of such an extension is given, the alleged universality has at most the status of an individual futural preference and does not amount to a public semantic necessity.

**Remark**

層級：公共語用層與決策層。  
用途：作為檢驗「普遍性」是否具有公共正當性的基本原則，用於分析政策、規範、成功學與宗教宣稱。

---

### Lemma P2（個人普遍性與公共普遍性的區分）

**中文敘述**

可區分兩種不同層級的普遍性：  
(1) 個人普遍性：在單一主體的時間結構與實踐中，某節點對所有 relevant 經驗具有 T1–T3 意義下的普遍性。  
(2) 公共普遍性：在一個場域內，多個主體在面對某類情況時，其 future-structure 可以被共同描述為遵守某一類似的 factorization 與語義泛性骨架。  
混淆兩者，將個人普遍性逕行當作公共普遍性，是語義濫用的一般形式。

**English formulation**

A distinction must be drawn between two levels of universality:  
(1) Individual universality: within the temporal structure and practices of a single subject, a node has the universality of Lemmas T1–T3 for all relevant experiences.  
(2) Public universality: within a field, multiple subjects facing a given type of situation can have their future-structures jointly described as following a similar pattern of factorization and semantic genericity.  
Conflating the two, and treating individual universality as if it were public universality, is a common form of semantic misuse.

**Remark**

層級：方法論層。  
用途：為 P1 提供技術分層，避免在分析時把「個人的深刻體驗」直接拔高為「對所有人的普遍真理」。

---

### Lemma P3（決策語句的未來性責任）

**中文敘述**

凡涉及制度設計、政策制定、醫療與教育實務等決策語句，若以「長遠」「未來」「社會整體」為理由訴諸某種普遍性，則該語句需承擔以下最小責任：  
(1) 指明其所假定的 future-structure（對誰的未來、在何種場域、以何種時間尺度）。  
(2) 說明此 future-structure 如何不只是單一階級、群體或人格結構的特例化。  
(3) 標示其適用域與失效條件。  
未滿足此三點者，皆不構成有資格壓迫異議者的「普遍性理由」。

**English formulation**

For decision statements in institutional design, policy making, medical or educational practice that appeal to "the long term", "the future", or "society as a whole" in order to invoke some form of universality, the following minimal responsibilities apply:  
(1) Specify the assumed future-structure (whose future, in which field, on what temporal scale).  
(2) Explain how this future-structure is not merely a special case tied to a single class, group, or personality structure.  
(3) Indicate its domain of validity and conditions of breakdown.  
Failing these, such appeals do not qualify as universal reasons that may legitimately override dissent.

**Remark**

層級：決策實務層。  
用途：將 P1–P2 的原則落地為可操作的審計項目，適用於分析具體政策文本與專業權威論述。

## 5. Family C — Connection to the Semantic Genericity Pipeline

(1) 實踐與目標
